# DS Learning — Hindi Course Blueprint (1 Year · 4 Terms)

**Who:** two ~8-year-old absolute beginners (heritage learners).
**What:** colloquial, conversational Hindi — **speaking & listening first**;
Devanagari **recognition only** (no handwriting this year).
**How:** live tutoring **+** the Language Matrix app (the app is the practice &
engagement engine; the tutor provides real conversation and judgement).
**Aligned to:** current NCERT (NCF-SE 2023) — *Sarangi* (Cl 1–2) foundations →
*Veena* (Cl 3) themes. See [CURRICULUM.md](CURRICULUM.md).

> **Format:** 4 Australian school terms ≈ **10 weeks each (~38–40 weeks)**,
> assuming **~2 sessions/week** of 45–60 min. Dates below are *relative* (Week 1…)
> — say the word and I'll pin them to your actual term calendar.

---

## Year outcome (where they land by the end)

By the end of the year each child can, **out loud and by ear**:

- Greet, introduce themselves, say how they feel.
- Name & describe ~**250–300 everyday words** (people, things, food, animals, places).
- **Give and follow commands** (खाओ, रुको, इधर आओ…).
- **Ask and answer** simple questions (यह क्या है? तुम्हारा नाम क्या है? यह कहाँ है?).
- Hold a **short back-and-forth conversation** (8–10 lines) about themselves,
  family, food and their day.
- Pronounce the tricky contrasts **intelligibly** (k/kh, t/th, short/long vowels).
- **Recognise ~40 high-frequency Devanagari words** + vowels and common consonants.

≈ **pre-A1 → A1 (spoken)** on a CEFR-style scale. Reading/writing is a *next-year* goal.

## Term 1 — "First Words & Sounds" (Survival Hindi)

**Goal:** break the ice, tune the ear, name the world around them, say *"this is a…"*.

| Wk | Focus | App / activity |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Entry diagnostic** + नमस्ते, names, हाँ/नहीं | Secret Agents + ★ baseline; Sounds (vowels) |
| 2–3 | R1 Colours & Counting; numbers 1–10 (oral) | Command-A-Robot, Cauldron; Sounds k/kh |
| 4–5 | R2 Family & Body | Secret Agents (यह माँ है), Robot; Sounds g/gh |
| 6–7 | R3 Animals & Home | all three games |
| 8 | G3-1 Greetings & Myself | Robot + conversation |
| 9 | **Revision** (spiral R1–G3-1, weakest first) | mixed |
| 10 | **Checkpoint:** name 20 things + greet & introduce yourself | live showcase + ★ |

**Can-do by end of Term 1:** greet, give their name, say यह … है for ~20 objects,
count 1–10 by ear, say a colour. Vowel length + k/kh heard and copied.

|---|---|---|
| 1 | Recap T1 + intro adjectives | Cauldron (adj+noun); Sounds ch/chh |
| 2–3 | G3-2 My Family (यह मेरा/मेरी … है) | Secret Agents, Robot; Sounds j/jh |

| 6–7 | G3-5 Food & Taste (खाओ/पियो) | Robot (adj+noun+verb); Sounds d/dh |
| 8 | G3-6 My Day & Actions | Robot + conversation |
| 9 | **Revision** | mixed |
| 10 | **Checkpoint:** describe 5 things + give/follow 5 commands | live + ★ |

**Can-do:** describe with adjective+noun (बड़ा कुत्ता), give & obey commands

(बड़ा/बड़ी) *heard* and *modelled* (not drilled).

## Term 3 — "Talk & Ask" (Conversations, questions, places)

**Goal:** make it two-way — ask questions, answer, locate things, say what someone *does*.

| Wk | Focus | App / activity |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Recap + question words क्या/कौन/कहाँ | Secret Agents; Sounds p/ph |
| 2–3 | G3-4 Animals & Sounds | Robot, Cauldron; Sounds b/bh |
| 4–5 | G3-7 Things & Places (में/पर/के पास) | Secret Agents; Sounds retroflex त/ट |
| 6–7 | G3-8 What? Who? Where? (full Q&A) | Secret Agents (core); nasal ं/ँ |
| 8 | **Feelings & Me\*** (खुश/उदास/भूखा) | Robot + conversation |
| 9 | **Revision** | mixed |
| 10 | **Checkpoint:** hold a 6-line ask-and-answer conversation | live + ★ |

**Can-do:** ask & answer यह क्या है? / यह कहाँ है? / तुम कौन हो?, place things
(मेज़ पर, बैग में), use simple present (माँ खाना बनाती है — modelled), and sustain
a short exchange with the tutor.

## Term 4 — "Put It Together" (Connected speech + reading-recognition)

**Goal:** join it up into connected talk about themselves; begin sight-reading.

| Wk | Focus | App / activity |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Recap + "tell me about your day" | all; Sounds review |
| 2–3 | **Nature & Weather\*** (मौसम, बारिश, धूप) | Robot, Cauldron |
| 4–5 | **Festivals & Culture\*** (त्योहार, फूलदेई — Veena U2) | conversation |
| 6–7 | **Body & Health\*** (intro past: खाया, गया) | Secret Agents, Robot |
| 8 | **Reading-recognition**: ~40 sight-words + vowels/consonants | recognition activity\* |
| 9 | **Revision** (whole year, spiral) | mixed |
| 10 | **End-of-year Showcase** + recognition check + report | live + ★ report |

**Can-do:** give a connected 8–10 line account of themselves / their day / their
family; understand and answer everyday questions; recognise ~40 written words and
the vowels + ~12 consonants. Ready for a **Grade-4 / Veena** literacy ramp next year.

## How a single session runs (~45–60 min)

1. **Warm-up (5m)** — *Sounds* tab: drill the term's sound pair (k vs kh…).
2. **Recall (5m)** — quick ★-logged review of last session's words (Secret Agents).
3. **New input (10m)** — tutor introduces new words with picture + audio (inventory).
4. **Game (15m)** — Command-A-Robot / Magic Cauldron / Secret Agents on the unit,
   with the **real mic** doing the speaking check.
5. **Real conversation (10m)** — tutor ↔ child, unscripted, using the new pattern.
   *This is where the actual learning is cemented — the app feeds it.*
6. **Wrap & log (5m)** — ★ rating + note per child; one tiny "listen at home" task.

---

## Assessment & progress

- **Diagnostic** (Term 1, Wk 1) → sets each child's starting unit.
- **Formative** (every session) → Teacher bar ★ + note, stamped with mode + unit.
- **Term checkpoint** (Wk 10) → the milestone task, scored on the rubric.
- **End-of-year** → Showcase + a one-page report on the 4 strands.

**Rubric (1–5★):** Listening · Speaking · Reading-recognition · Engagement.
**Progress signal:** cumulative words "owned" + a can-do checklist per term.

### Pronunciation roadmap (Sounds tab)
T1 vowels + k/kh, g/gh → T2 ch/chh, j/jh, t/th, d/dh → T3 p/ph, b/bh, retroflex
त/ट · द/ड, nasal ं/ँ → T4 review + sentence rhythm.

### Reading-recognition roadmap
T1 pure oral (script shown, not taught) → T2 *notice* the script under each word →
T3 recognise vowels + first consonants → T4 ~40 sight-words + ~12 consonants.

## Gaps — what still needs building for this plan

To deliver the full year, these are the outstanding app builds (in rough priority):

1. **New units** — Feelings & Me, Nature & Weather, Festivals & Culture, Body &
   Health (Term 3–4). Easy to add to `src/data/curriculum.js`.
2. **Receptive listening game** — "tap the word you hear" (trains *listening*,
   complements the speaking games).
3. **Spaced-repetition recall** — resurface words a child got wrong; without this,
   retention leaks between sessions.
4. **Per-child progress tracking** — currently one shared ★ log; needs a profile
   per child + a words-owned tally.
5. **Pre-recorded audio clips** — guaranteed Hindi audio independent of the device
   voice (`speech.js` already supports `block.audioUrl`).
6. **Grade-4 / Veena literacy ramp** — next year: matras (barahkhadi), guided reading.

None of these block starting Term 1 with what's built today.
